Soel Farm

Soel Farm is a family-run business based in Millstone, New Jersey. The owners are professionals who, at the peak of their careers decided to move to a farm in New Jersey, transforming their enthusiastic hobby into a new way of living. Soel Farm specializes in microgreens, which are young seedlings of vegetables, herbs, beans and grains. Harvested after 2-4 weeks, they are exceptionally nutritional and provide excellent source of health-supporting phytonutrients. Despite their small size, microgreens have surprisingly intense flavors and are used as a visual enhancement and flavor accent. Fine dining restaurants favor microgreens to enhance both the creative presentation and flavor of their dishes.
